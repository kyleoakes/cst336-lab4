Welcome to Mercury!

Mercury is the planet closest to the Sun. Mercury is about 35.385 million miles from the Sun and about 73.292 million miles from Earth as of 7/18/2020. During the day, the surface of Mercury can reach 800 degrees Fahrenhrit, and at night it can reach 290 degrees Fahrenheit. Mercury has a very thin atmosphere containing hydrogen, helium, oxygen, sodium, calcium, potassium, and water vapor. Mercury is named after the Roman messenger god of commerce, eloquence, messages, communication, and travelers.
